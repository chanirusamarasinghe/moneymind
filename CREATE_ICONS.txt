Instructions to create PWA icons:

1. Go to: https://favicon.io/favicon-generator/
2. In "Text" field type: MM
3. Background: Rounded
4. Background color: #4f8ef7 (blue)
5. Font color: #ffffff (white)
6. Font size: 80
7. Click "Generate"
8. Download the zip file
9. Inside the zip, find favicon.ico, apple-touch-icon.png, favicon-32x32.png
10. Create a folder called "icons" inside money-manager/
11. Copy apple-touch-icon.png to icons/ and rename to icon-192.png
12. Also copy it as icon-512.png

OR just skip icons — the app works fine without them!
The PWA will still be installable, just without a custom icon.
